About

A data engineering company designed by engineers who understand the field

MOORIM EDS is built on more than 20 years of OEM engineering experience, with engineers who understand real field conditions designing the data structure directly.

Rather than delivering software in isolation, we define what data is needed in context and implement systems that can actually capture it on site.

Our differentiation comes from understanding the relationship between physical phenomena and data, then solving problems in measurable terms.

Core capabilities

  • 20+ years of OEM-based field engineering experience
  • Mechanical systems understanding combined with software execution
  • Design starting from acquisition architecture, not only analysis
  • Integrated hardware and software systems for real deployment

Value

Customers often begin without knowing what data they should even be measuring. We define the right signals from real field problems and build the system required to capture them.
