About

MOORIM EDS is a data-focused engineering company designed by engineers who understand field constraints, backed by over 20 years of OEM engineering experience.

We go beyond software delivery: we define the required data in context and implement practical systems that can actually capture it in the field.

Unlike software-first vendors, MOORIM EDS understands the relationship between physical phenomena and data—and solves problems in measurable terms.

Core capabilities

  • 20+ years of OEM-based field engineering experience
  • Mechanical systems understanding + software engineering execution
  • Design from acquisition architecture—not only analysis
  • Integrated hardware + software implementations for real deployment

Value we deliver
