About

Data specialists designed directly by engineers who understand the field

Based on more than 20 years of OEM engineering experience, MOORIM T.C. has engineers who understand the field directly design the data structure.

Grounded in mechanical systems knowledge and real testing and evaluation experience, we go beyond simple software delivery to define required field data and implement systems that can actually acquire it.

Our differentiation lies in understanding the relationship between physical phenomena and data, then solving problems in measurable forms.

Core capabilities

  • More than 20 years of OEM-based field engineering experience
  • Integrated capability combining mechanical systems understanding with software technology
  • Design starting from data acquisition architecture, not only analysis
  • Integrated hardware and software implementation for real field deployment

Value
